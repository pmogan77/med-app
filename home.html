How It Works

Symptoms

Enter your symptoms via our 3d model user interface. The region on the 3d model will pertain the the region the symptom is most commonly found

Diagnosis

After entering your name into the website, you will receive a diagnosis that best matches the given symptoms.

Treatment

You will receive a personalized treatment plan based off of your symptoms

Recovery

After finding your personalized treatment based off of our web clinic database, we wish you the best of luck in your recover.
